Lecturify

TDT4140 is a project based subject in Computer Science 2nd grade at NTNU. We where devided into pseudorandom groups based on computing skills. We were informed that we would make a PekkaBot and, we would get more information later.

Motivation
Despite of technological advancements made in the past 50 years, the university education has been quite stable for hundreds of years with its frontal lectures, exercises, assignments and grading. We believe that it is time to change this! With the advancement of Artificial Intelligence and Big Data, automation will be a more visible part of our life in the near future. This assignment is the kick-off for the TDT4140 project.

Project description
 We where asked to interview a couple of lecturers and/or professors. Either at IDI, NTNU or outside to find a problem to fix. We would ask the lecturer/professors about their top-5 problems they are currently facing in their teacher endeavors. In particular we where interested in problems that could be automated.
